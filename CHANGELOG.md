## [Unreleased]

### Added
- Initial module implementation for HiiRetail IAM resources
- Support for custom role management
- Support for business unit resource management
- Support for group management
- Support for role binding management
- Automatic group generation based on business units and custom roles
- Automatic role binding generation for auto-generated groups
- YAML-driven configuration support
- Comprehensive examples (complete, simple, manual)
- Full module documentation
- Input validation for custom role stages
